Movie Theater Ticket Reservation Application



Developed a ticket reservation system in Java where registered and guest users can reserve tickets for movies. Users can search and select movies, pick a showtime, view available seats graphically, make payments and receive a confirmation of bookings by email. Unsatisfied, a user can also cancel their booking and receive a refund.

Hear It Here



Created a music forum site that allows users to interact and share their thoughts on various music-related topics. The forum allows users to create posts and share their thoughts through comments on other users' posts. Additionally, users have the freedom to personalize their profiles with ease according to their preferences. Hear It Here is a fun and easy way to expand your musical horizons and connect with other fans.
